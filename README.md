#                                                Technology and Information Systems

---

# Course Synopsis

This course introduces students to the fundamental concepts of technology and information systems and their applications in modern organizations. It covers the role of information systems in supporting business processes, decision-making, and strategic goals. Students will learn about hardware, software, networking, databases, cybersecurity, and emerging technologies, as well as how to manage and utilize information effectively. The course also emphasizes practical skills through activities such as e-portfolio creation, GitHub usage, and basic IT problem-solving, preparing students to apply technological solutions in real-world scenarios.
